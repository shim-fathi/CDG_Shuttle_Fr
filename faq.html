Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your Paris airport transfer with CDG Shuttle.

Booking Process

Booking is quick and easy. Click the "Book Now" button on any page on our website. Fill in your journey details (pickup location, drop-off location, date, time, number of passengers), select your vehicle type, enter your contact details, and click "Check Price & Availability". You'll receive an instant price and can confirm your booking. A confirmation email will be sent to you within a few minutes.
Absolutely. You can call us 24/7 on +33 (0)1 43 32 70 81 or +33 6 09 71 35 76 to speak with one of our team and book your transfer over the phone. Alternatively, email us at contact@groupbluevanparis.com and we'll respond within 2 hours.
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee vehicle availability. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings. Contact us directly for urgent requests and we'll do our best to help.
Yes, every booking receives an email confirmation with full journey details, your driver's name, vehicle information, and contact number. You'll also receive a reminder the day before your pickup.

Pricing & Payment

All our prices are 100% fixed. The price you see at booking is the price you pay — there are no meters running, no surge pricing, and no hidden extras. Tolls, airport surcharges, and VAT are all included in the quoted price.
We accept payment by cash (Euros), all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and bank transfer for corporate accounts. Payment can be made on the day or in advance at booking — whichever you prefer.
Yes, all motorway tolls, airport pick-up/drop-off charges, and applicable taxes are included in your quoted price. You will never be asked to pay any extra charges on the day of travel.
A night supplement of 15% applies to transfers between 22:00 and 06:00. This will be clearly shown in your booking price — there are no surprises on the day. All other surcharges (if applicable) are always disclosed upfront.

Pickup & Drop-off

Your driver will meet you in the arrivals hall with a name board displaying your name. At Charles de Gaulle, we meet in the designated meet & greet areas of your terminal. At Orly, we meet at the arrivals exit. You'll receive your driver's contact number in your confirmation email so you can reach them directly if needed.
We track all flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver automatically adjusts their arrival time accordingly — at absolutely no extra charge to you. You do not need to call us; we handle it proactively. Simply collect your luggage and your driver will be waiting for you.
At airports, we include 60 minutes of free waiting time from the scheduled landing time — giving you plenty of time to clear customs, collect luggage, and make your way to the arrivals hall. For non-airport pickups, 15 minutes of free waiting time is included.
Yes, absolutely. We offer genuine door-to-door service. Simply provide your full address or hotel name at booking and your driver will drop you right at the entrance. If you need multiple stops along the route, this can be arranged for a small supplement of €15 per additional stop.

Cancellation & Changes

We offer free cancellation for any booking cancelled more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made between 12 and 24 hours in advance incur a 50% charge. No-shows or cancellations with less than 12 hours' notice are charged at the full rate.
Yes, you can amend your booking (date, time, pickup address, vehicle type) up to 12 hours before your scheduled transfer at no charge, subject to availability. Simply call us on +33 (0)1 43 32 70 81 or email contact@groupbluevanparis.com.
Please contact us as soon as possible if you miss your flight. We will do our best to rebook your transfer for a later time or date. The standard amendment policy applies. We strongly recommend travel insurance that covers missed connections.

Special Requirements

Yes! Child seats (infant, toddler, and booster) are available at no extra charge. Please specify the age and weight of your child at booking so we can fit the correct seat. We follow all French road safety regulations regarding child restraint systems.
Yes, we can provide wheelchair-accessible vehicles on request. Please contact us directly when booking to discuss your specific requirements and we will ensure the right vehicle is arranged.
Absolutely — Disneyland Paris transfers are one of our most popular services. We run direct transfers from both CDG and Orly airports to all Disney hotels and the theme park entrance. Our spacious minivans are perfect for families with children. See our rates page for Disneyland prices.
Yes. Our minibuses can carry up to 16 passengers per vehicle. For groups larger than 16, we can coordinate multiple vehicles travelling together. Please contact us in advance for group bookings so we can ensure the best arrangements for your party.
